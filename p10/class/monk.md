### Level 1: Martial Arts
Through the understanding and control you have over your physical body, you gain the following benefits:
+ Your Improvised Martial Attacks inherently have the "*Off-Handed*" property.
+ Your Improvised Martial Attacks can have the "*Reach (X)*" or "*Thrown (X)*" properties, provided you make the Attack using a weapon with those properties and are Proficient with that weapon.
+ You can make an off-handed Attack after taking any type of Action on your turn, provided the Attack is Improvised.
+ You can make any number of off-handed attacks on your turn, provided they are all Improvised.

### Level 1: Centered Breath? Breathing Technique?
You can use 3 Actions on your turn to take a deep breath and regain HP equal to half your Maximum HP.<br>

### Level 1: Mark of the Palm?
You can use 1 Action on your turn to make an Improvised Strike against a creature within your Reach. On a hit, increase the Degree of Success for that Attack by 1 and add a Mark that it must bear for a minute.<br>
You add a +1 bonus to any Martial Attack you make against a creature bearing at least one Mark.<br>
Whenever you hit a creature within your Reach with an Improvised Strike and that creature already bears at least one Mark, you can remove any number of Marks to push that creature 1 pace directly away from you for each removed Mark. A creature moved this way will also fall down where it lands.
