### Level 1: Pact Boon
You have recieved a Boon from a greater entity, in the shape of a physical object, intended to either aid you in fulfilling your end of the bargain or as a means of fulfilling its end of the bargain to you. Regardless of its nature as an object, you can use your Boon as a focus to cast any Spell you know.<br>
Choose one of the following benefits:

#### Boon of the Bauble
Your Boon is an accessory radiating an eldritch aura you must perpetually wear as no one, including yourself, is able to remove it from your person.<br>
You can perform an hour-long ritual to summon a *Familiar*. You can only have one familiar at a time. (PHRASING? DETAILS!)<br>
Your Familiar is a Tiny creature of the same type as the entity that gave you the Boon. Its Level is the same as yours, each of its Attributes is 0, and it has 4 Walk Speed and 4 Fly Speed.<br>
It understands all languages you do (though it can't speak), shares all your Training, and has the same Learned Skills as you do.<br>
In an Action Scene, it has 1 Action each round, but can move once before using it on its turn. (PHRASING?)<br>
Your Familiar has no MP of its own, instead sharing access to yours.<br><br>

Your Familiar can use 1 Action on its turn to make a Magic Range Attack with a range of 5 paces, dealing 1* damage on a hit. You choose the type for this damage when you gain this feature.<br><br>

If Warlock is your main Class, you also gain the following benefits:<br>
+ You belong to the Expert Class Group.
+ You learn any 2 Skills.

#### Boon of the Blade
Your Boon is an Advanced Melee Weapon of your choice, imbued with great power and inexorably bound to you.<br>
You can use 1 Action on your turn to summon your Boon into your hand from wherever it is.<br><br>

If Warlock is your main Class, you also gain the following benefits:<br>
+ You belong to the Warrior Class Group.
+ You become Proficient with Advanced Melee Weapons and Heavy Armor.

#### Boon of the Book
Your Boon is a tome full of otherworldly glyphs and pictures that only you can comprehend.<br>
You can perform a 1 minute ritual reading your Boon to ask a question directed towards the entity that gifted it to you. Its answer to your question appears within the Boon's pages. Once you've done so, you can't do so again until you've finished a Long Rest. (PHRASING?)<br>
You can perform a minute-long ritual to summon a new copy of your Boon into your hands, causing the original to disintegrate, wherever it is.<br><br>

If Warlock is your main Class, you also gain the following benefits:<br>
+ You belong to the Mage Class Group.
+ You learn any two Spells.
