**Prerequisite:** *Sworn Enemy*.<br>

### Level 1: Enhancement? Rite
You can use 1 Action on your turn to enhance up to two weapons you are holding. You can use a weapon you've enhanced this way as though it was any type of Focus. The enhancement remains for a minute, until you let go of the weapon, or you use this ability again.<br>
When you enhance the weapon, choose one Elemental or Spiritual damage type. When you deal damage with the enhaced weapon, you may change any Physical damage dealt to the type you chose.

### Level 1: Sixth Sense
**Prerequisite:** *Sworn Enemy*.<br>
You can sense if there are any Sworn Enemies, or a lingering effect caused by one, within "the current league/area". (PHRASING? DETAILS?)<br>
You can use 1 Action on your turn to detect the exact position of each Sworn Enemy within 100 paces of you.

### Level 2: Nowhere to Hide
**Prerequisite:** *Baleful Brand*.<br>
While a creature is Branded by you, it gains no benefit from being concealed by an illusory effect (including disguises and invisibility. PHRASING?) and it automatically fails any Test it makes to hide itself or conceal its actions.<br>
If it attempts to magically alter its form in any way (except for returning to its true form), it must make a Will Save against your Magic Might. On a failed Save, the attempt to change its form fails and the creature is Stunned until the end of your next turn.

### Level 3: Hardened Heart
**Prerequisite:** *Sworn Enemy*.<br>
You gain Advantage on any Save a Sworn Enemy forces you to make.

### Level 4: Nowhere to Run
**Prerequisite:** *Baleful Brand*.<br>
While a creature is Branded by you, any Speed it has is reduced to 1 pace.<br>
If it attempts to teleport or leave its current plane by any means, it must make a Will Save against your Magic Might. On a failed Save, the attempt to teleport or leave the plane fails and the creature is Stunned until the end of your next turn.

### Level 5: Sealed Fate
**Prerequisite:** *Sworn Enemy*.<br>
When you make a Martial Attack against a Sworn Enemy and miss, you may instead treat the result as a Baseline Success.<br>
If you do so, any damage dealt by the Attack is halved.
