# Rogue
Slippery tricksters evading traps and devestation alike.<br>
Highly capable mercenaries and true masters of their craft.<br>
Conniving experts that can find and exploit any weakness in a foe or their plans.<br>

## Class Properties
+ **Class Group:** Expert
+ **HP Bonus:** 2
+ **MP Bonus:** 2

## Starting Proficiencies
+ **Weapons:** Simple Melee. Simple Ranged. Specific (Pistols).
+ **Armor:** Light.
+ **Shields:** None.
+ **Focus:** None.

## Starting Knowledge
+ **Skills:** Four of the following: Athletics, Guts, Acrobatics, Handling, Finesse, Stealth, Performance, Suggestion, Crafting, Investigation, Lore, Medicine.
+ **Languages:** Two common.
+ **Training:** One occupation. One gaming set. One vehicle.
+ **Spells:** None.

### Level 1: Sneak Attack!
When you hit a creature with a Martial Attack that was made with Advantage and without Disadvantage, you can increase the Physical damage dealt by an amount equal to 2 times your Skill Bonus.<br>
After doing so, you can't do so against the same creature again until you've entered a new Action Scene.

### Level 1: Opportunist
Whenever you make a Martial Attack against a creature, you can do so with Advantage, provided that at least one of the following conditions is being met:
 + The targeted creature is Downed.
 + The targeted creature is Grappled.
 + The targeted creature was unaware of your position.
 + Your Attack against the target creature is a Sudden Strike.
 + An allied creature has the target creature within its reach on the opposite side from where you are attacking it.

### Level 1: Thinking it Through
When you make a Test of Skill or an Attack on your turn, you may use 1 additional Action to prevent it from gaining Disadvantage or being Interruptible.

### Level 1: Giving the Slip
When you are hit by an Attack, you can use 1 Action to reduce any damage you would take by half.<br>
When you make a Save against taking damage, you can use 1 Action to instead take half as much damage. If the damage has already been halved by you succeeding on the Save, you instead take no damage.
